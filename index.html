Unicorn Badminton Club

Our Club is a small, friendly club of league and club players, students, junior players and coaches.

We really welcome visitors so please do come and try us out - the club is a great night out whether you are an experienced player, someone who played a long while back and is now interested in returning to badminton, or someone wanting to progress your play on from casual to a bit more competitive. We particularly welcome younger players (juniors and students) to help you enjoy and develop your game, and to gain an introduction to a lifetime of healthy, enjoyable sport.

Adults and Students: We play on Thursday nights:

• From 7:30pm to 9:30pm.

• Season dates: 4th September 2025 to 30th April 2026 inclusive (but excluding 27th December and some occasions when the sports hall is required by the school for examinations).

Juniors: We have two groups:

• Beginners (approx ages 8 to 12 yrs) play on Thursdays from 5:30pm to 6:30pm Season dates: 11th September 2025 to 30th April 2026 inclusive (but excluding the school Autumn/February half-term holidays and Christmas/Easter holidays, and occasional school use of the hall for exams).

How much does it cost to Play?

Adults and Students:

The subscription for the 2025/6 season is £105 (£75 for those in full-time education up to first degree level). For players joining part way through the season we will work out a pro-rata amount based on the number of sessions remaining through to the end of the season. Visitors/occasional players are welcome; this costs £7 a night (£5 for players in full-time education up to first degree level). If you play as a visitor more than 6 times in the season, we ask you to join as a club member and take out a subscription - anything you have paid as a visitor will be deducted from the subscription due. We strongly advise players to take out membership of Badminton England ...... which gives you various benefits and insurance covers - click here for more details. The Club's own insurance only covers the Club's liabilities; it does not provide cover or benefits for individual club members or visitors.

Juniors:

The subscription for players in the 'Beginners' group is £45 for the season; and for those in the 'Improvers' group is £65. We are happy for new players to try out the club to see how they enjoy our sessions; the cost is £3 per session (Beginners group)/£5 (Improvers group) for up to 6 sessions, after which we ask players to take out a full membership (pro rata for the rest of the season and minus the amounts paid so far).

Payments:

It helps us greatly if payments are made by bank transfer directly to our account rather than as cash; please ask any of the coaches or committee members for details.
